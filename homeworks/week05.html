1. Measures of Location / Central Tendency

These are statistics that attempt to summarise a data set by a single value around which the data “centre” or “cluster”.

a) Arithmetic Mean

Definition.

For a sample of values x_1, x_2, …, x_n, the arithmetic mean is

\( \bar x = \frac{1}{n} \sum_{i=1}^n x_i \)
